Acids, Bases, and the pH Scale

The pH scale measures the acidity or basicity of a substance. Substances with a pH less than 7 are **acidic**, substances with a pH of 7 are **neutral**, and those with a pH greater than 7 are **basic**. Here's a list of common substances and their pH levels.
